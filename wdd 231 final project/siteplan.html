Cute & Covered Site Plan

Site Name

Cute & Covered

The name was chosen to reflect the store's focus on selling modest yet fashionable clothing, appealing to the target audience who value both style and modesty.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Cute & Covered website is to provide an online platform where customers can browse and purchase modest clothing that adheres to the dressing standards of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints. The site will showcase various types of clothing including shirts, pants, dresses, swimwear, and deals.

Scenarios

  • What are the latest modest fashion trends?
  • How can I contact customer support for order issues?

Color Schema

The selected color schema includes the following colors:

  • #8afdff - This color will be used for headings and accents.
  • #3b3b3b - This color will be used for main text and background elements.
  • #fdbfc9 - This color will be used for buttons and highlights.
